If you're interested in becoming an ACM officer, come to the meeting at 8am Dec 7 at JFSB B003! Breakfast will be provided. Or, email us at cs.byu.acm@gmail.com.
Any of the following roles can be filled:

  • Events


    Ensure events run smoothly. Duties include management of purchases, prizes, food, attendance statistics, room and tech reservations, high sarcasm, and pirate and ninja challenge creation and maintenance.

  • Service


    Plan and coordinate service projects for ACM members. Possible events include tutoring and events for K-12 programmers, mentoring for BYU students majoring in tech fields, and open source coding projects.

  • Finance


    The finance officer is in charge of keeping a current budget of the ACM club funds. The officer decides if money should be spent or not. The best position if you ever wanted to test your accounting skills.
